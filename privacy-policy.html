Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

Lokal is a mobile app that helps you discover cultural events — concerts, exhibitions, festivals, theater, and more — happening near you. It's built and operated by Stephen Miko as an independent project.

This policy explains what data Lokal collects, how it's used, and your rights. We've kept it short and straightforward.

Information We Collect

Location data: When you use Lokal, we request access to your device's GPS location. This stays on your device and is sent to our server only to find events near you. We don't store your location on our servers or track your movements over time.

Usage data: We may collect basic, anonymous usage information — like which cities are most popular or how often the app is opened — to help us improve Lokal. This data is not tied to your identity.

No accounts, no personal info: Lokal doesn't require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information like your name or email address.

How We Use Your Information

  • To show you cultural events happening near your current location
  • To improve the app's content, coverage, and reliability
  • To understand which cities and event types are most useful to our users

We don't sell your data. We don't use it for advertising. That's not what Lokal is about.

Third-Party Services

Lokal uses a few third-party services to work:

  • Mapbox — provides the map you see in the app. Mapbox receives your approximate location to render map tiles. See Mapbox's privacy policy.
  • Ticketmaster and local event sources — we pull event data from public event listings and APIs (Ticketmaster, city tourism portals, and cultural guides) to build our events database. These services don't receive any of your data.
  • Claude AI (Anthropic) — we use AI to write helpful editorial notes about events. This happens on our servers during data processing — your device never communicates with Anthropic directly.

Data Storage

Event data (titles, descriptions, dates, venues, editorial notes) is stored on our servers. Your device fetches this data when you open the app.

Since Lokal doesn't have user accounts, we don't store any personal data on our servers. Your location is used in real time to query for nearby events and is not saved.

Children's Privacy

Lokal is suitable for users of all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children (or anyone else). Since the app doesn't require accounts or collect personal data, there's no age-restricted information involved.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's not much to request access to or delete. But if you have any questions or concerns about your privacy while using Lokal, we're happy to hear from you.

You can revoke location access at any time through your device's settings. The app will still work — you'll just need to browse events by searching for a city instead.

Changes to This Policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. For significant changes, we may also include a notice in the app.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at privacy@localevents.com.
